coconut-lentil-curry

Description

This lentil recipe is an eye opener - an incredible creamy coconut Lentil Curry made with just TWO spices!

It's a streamlined version of Indian Dal, delivering a similar intoxicating curry flavour but a whole lot less complicated.

Ingredients

  • 50g (3 tbsp) butter , unsalted (sub 2 tbsp neutral oil)
  • 4 garlic cloves, finely minced
  • 1.5 tbsp ginger, finely minced
  • 1 onion , finely chopped (white, yellow, brown)
  • 2 tbsp curry powder , mild or spicy (your choice!)
  • 1/2 tsp tumeric powder
  • 1 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup dried lentils
  • 400 ml / 14 oz coconut milk , full fat
  • 400g / 14 oz canned tomato , crushed or diced
  • 3 cups (750 ml) water
